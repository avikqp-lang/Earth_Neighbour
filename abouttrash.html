About Our Project

Our eco-friendly trash bin is a simple yet impactful solution designed to promote cleaner surroundings and responsible waste disposal. Created using easily available materials such as cardboard, plastic containers, or lightweight metal sheets, this bin demonstrates how small innovations can contribute to a sustainable lifestyle. It is compact, durable, and suitable for use in homes, classrooms, offices, and small public spaces. The bin encourages proper waste segregation by providing a clearly marked space for disposing of everyday waste items. Its lightweight construction makes it easy to move, while its sturdy design ensures long-lasting use. By using recyclable or reusable materials for its construction, the bin reduces environmental impact and supports the idea of creating useful products from everyday items. Our goal is to spread awareness about maintaining hygiene and managing waste effectively. This trash bin is not just a product—it is an initiative to inspire responsible habits and contribute to a cleaner, greener community.
