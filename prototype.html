“Earth Neighbour converts what you throw away into something that gives life back to the planet.”

The SmartBin prototype—designed under the concept of Earth Neighbour—is an innovative, educational, and eco-friendly waste system that demonstrates how organic waste can be transformed into nutrient-rich compost. The prototype features a transparent manure collection chamber, an LED-based heating indicator, and an ultrasonic fill-level sensor that tracks how full the bin is in real time. It also uses a QR-linked dashboard to provide users with insights into their composting progress, token rewards, and environmental impact. This compact model not only helps students and communities understand the science of composting but also encourages responsible waste disposal through smart technology and visual feedback. Simple to build, cost-efficient, and interactive, the SmartBin prototype makes sustainability easier to learn, practice, and adopt in everyday life.
